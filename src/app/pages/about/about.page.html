WHO IS HAYDA?

HAYDA, headquartered at Rancho Cucamonga, California is a creative mobile application and webpage that helps Uninsured Young Adults (UYA) 19-34 years who do not have access to preventive health care to take ownership of their health to decrease risks for diseases like a stroke, diabetes, and hypertension.

Preventable diseases like a stroke, diabetes, and hypertension are on the rise in this group according to research, so HAYDA’s purpose is to disrupt this pattern with a convenient, cost-efficient, and collated educational information that is imbedded in the app with the option of video conferencing with a health coach or health navigator.

It’s a unique platform that provides easy access to routine health care information and resource similar to what they would receive at a clinic visit with a primary health care provider.

HAYDA will offer a wide variety of healthy lifestyle recommendation from the health industry’s most trustworthy organization, experts, top-rated lifestyle sources to educate, empower, and engage UYA in preventive health lifestyle. The core information will focus on controlling weight, blood pressure, and blood glucose. Recommendation include:

  • Fitness
  • Nutrition
  • Blood Pressure
  • Weight
  • Blood Glucose
  • Mindfulness

HAYDA is led by Linda Dankwa, a Registered Nurse who has been in the healthcare industry for 30 years, with an MBA focusing on Healthcare Management, and a Doctorate in Social Work with an emphasis in social innovation. Linda Dankwa has an in-depth knowledge of preventive health lifestyle demands as well as the needs of Uninsured Young Adults (UYA).

The team includes the Chief Executive Officer (CEO), Chief Financial Officer (CFO), Chief Information Officer (CIO), Marketer, Content Manager, and a Health Coach. The team’s goals will consist of strategic development for mobile app focusing on preventive health lifestyle, day-to-day operations, and business management.

It was apparent to Linda Dankwa that uninsured young adults were showing an increase in the rate of preventable diseases, and an unserved market need existed for this product catering to preventive health lifestyle. Furthermore, after researching the market and literature, this theory was proven. This product caters to Uninsured Young Adults (UYA) ages 19-34, but it will be useful for anyone interested in health promotion.

Vision for HAYDA

We Aim to Educate, Encourage, Engage, and Empower Uninsured Young Adults to Embrace Preventive Health Lifestyle to promote Longevity in our Communities.

Mission for HAYDA

Through the Use of Mobile Health and Medical Technology Applications, Uninsured Young Adults Will Have Access to Aggregated Health Information and Resources on a Platform that Incorporates Their Preferences and Goals to Provide Meaningful Feedback and Suggestions to Engage them in Preventive Health Lifestyle.
